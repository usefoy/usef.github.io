About me

  • Currently I hold the position of assistant profesor at the Department of Computer Technology, Abha College of Technology, Abha, Asir, Saudi Arabia. Prior to this role, I completed my Ph.D degree in Computer Science at Kansas State University, Manhattan, KS, USA. My doctoral research focused on image encryption, security, and real time systems Before pursuing my Ph.D., I earned my Master degree in Information Technology from North Carolina A&T University, Greensboro, NC, USA, and I received my Bachelor degree in Information Technology from King AbdulAziz University, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
  • Scope of research: Image Encryption, Security, Cyber Physical Systems, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, Real Time Systems
