Course Description:

Internet of Things is a new revolution of the Internet that is rapidly gathering momentum driven by the advancements in sensor networks, mobile devices, and wireless communications, networking and cloud technologies. In this specialized course, students will learn the importance of IoT in society, current components of typical IoT devices and trends for the future. IoT design considerations, constraints and interfacing between the physical world and devices will also be covered. In addition, it also covers key components of networking to ensure that students understand how to connect their devices to the Internet.
